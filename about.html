About Untrace AI

Untrace AI is designed to tackle financial literacy challenges through interactive, gamified learning. Our mission is to empower underserved communities and younger generations with knowledge to manage finances effectively.

By integrating AI, real-world simulations, and engaging quizzes, we aim to make financial education accessible and fun for all.
